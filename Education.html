Fun Projects

During exchange we embarked on a wood and innovation module, which required us to come up with an innovative idea made entirely of wood. This was because Sweden has a vast amount of wood due to their sustainable and economic use of their forests. My group wanted to do a consumer product and decided on wooden speakers. While there were many existing wooden speakers in the market,we realised many of them were quite bulky and there were no wooden horn speakers that were currently in the market. This was probably due to its bulky size and difficulty in bringing it around, as such my group decided to embark on a project to make a collapsible wooden horn speaker. As we did not have the material necessary, we simply used a thick piece of wood, and some magnets to detach the upper part of the horn speaker with the lower part. The entire idea is one that requires us to collapse the horn speaker piece by piece and assemble it together with magnets when in use, making it less bulky and at the same time fulfilling its function as a horn speaker.
